Menorist AI Tracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

This policy explains how Menorist AI Tracker collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the application and sign in with Google OAuth.

1. Data we collect

  • Google account email address.
  • Basic profile data from Google (display name and avatar URL).
  • Application data you create in Menorist AI Tracker (goals, profile fields, and activity metadata).

2. Why we use data

  • To authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • To provide Menorist AI Tracker core functionality (goal management, collaboration, and notifications).
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.

3. Data sharing

Menorist AI Tracker does not sell personal data. We do not share Google user data with third parties except when required by law or necessary to provide infrastructure services.

4. Data retention and deletion

We store data only as long as needed for service operation and legal obligations. You can request account and data deletion by contacting support.

If you have privacy questions, contact us at antonzaratustra@gmail.com.
