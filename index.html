What Mentorist is

Mentorist is a social goal-organizer that helps people and communities set goals, keep life balance, and move forward with mentors and peers.

  • Goal constructor with SMARTER principles and shared accountability.
  • Balance wheel for key life spheres and progress visualization.
  • Mentor/mentee collaboration, feedback, and community activity flows.
